How Schoolify Works

Get your school running digitally in three simple steps. No technical expertise required - our team supports you at every stage.

1
Setup Your School

Register your school, configure classes, sections, subjects, and staff profiles. Import existing student data in minutes. Our support team assists with onboarding.

2
Manage Daily Operations

Take attendance, collect fees, schedule exams, communicate with parents via WhatsApp and SMS, generate ID cards, and manage payroll - all from one dashboard.

3
Generate Reports & Analytics

Produce professional result cards, fee ledgers, attendance summaries, and school performance analytics instantly. Share reports with parents and administrators digitally.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Schoolify School Management Software before getting started.

School management software (also called School ERP) is a cloud-based platform that helps schools automate daily operations - student admissions, fee collection, attendance tracking, exam scheduling, result cards, staff payroll, and parent communication - from one centralized system.
Schoolify is a cloud-based School Management Software (School ERP) designed for schools, academies, and coaching centers worldwide. It automates fee collection, attendance tracking, result card generation, ID card printing, payroll, and parent communication via WhatsApp and SMS.
Yes. Schoolify is a cloud-based platform accessible worldwide. Schools in the USA, UK, UAE, Canada, Australia, India, Malaysia, South Africa, and other countries can use Schoolify to manage their school operations from anywhere.
Schoolify includes: Student Information System, automated fee collection and reminders, attendance management with WhatsApp/SMS alerts, exam and result management, school ID card generator, certificate generator, timetable management, staff payroll, parent communication portal, school website builder, and real-time analytics dashboards.
Schoolify is fully cloud-based. You can access it from any device - desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone - with no installation required. Data is securely backed up in the cloud automatically, so you never lose important school records.
Schoolify offers flexible plans starting from Rs. 999/month. Options include 1-month (Rs. 999), 3-month (Rs. 2,500), 6-month (Rs. 4,500), and 12-month (Rs. 8,000) plans. All plans include every feature. Contact us for international pricing.
Yes, Schoolify supports multi-campus management. Institutions with multiple branches can manage all campuses from one centralized dashboard, with separate data for each campus while maintaining a unified administrative view.
Yes, Schoolify offers a free live demo so schools can explore all features before subscribing. You can also access the live demo at app.schoolify.online. Contact us via WhatsApp (+92 301 898 1055) to book a guided demo session.
